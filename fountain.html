Windmills

Bangui, Ilocos Norte

The Bangui Wind Farm is a wind farm in Bangui, Ilocos Norte, Philippines. The wind farm uses 20 units of 70-meter (230 ft) high Vestas V82 1.65 MW wind turbines, arranged in a single row stretching along a 9-kilometer (5.6 mi) shoreline of Bangui Bay, facing the South China Sea.

Bell Tower Church

Bantay, Ilocos Sur

Bantay Church, located in Bantay, Ilocos Sur, is a Roman Catholic church in the Ilocos town of the same name. Also known as the Saint Augustine Parish Church, it’s one of the oldest surviving churches in the Philippines dating back to 1591. Bantay Church is also famed for its grand design and architecture inspired by Western and European cultures.
